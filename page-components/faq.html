FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What are Delphi Points ?
These are virtual points like Credit Card points, that can be used across any games in Delphi Land. These can be used to redeem cool gifts.
Can I transfer my Delphis to my friends ?
Absolutely. Sharing is Caring. Transfer some Delphis to Friends, and enjoy playing group games with them.
What are Insta Games ?
Every game is designed to be played in Public or Peer-to-Peer version. In P2P modes, you can create/host a game, invite friends over Whatsapp and other social media, and play in private groups. The private games are also played with Delphi Coins. Coolest Party in Town !
How do I earn Delphi Points ?
Delphi Points can be earned by simply playing the games on Delphi Land. Additionally, they can be earned by contributing to our public games like travel and music.
