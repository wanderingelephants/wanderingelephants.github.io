Frequenty Asked Questions
What is Metamask?
Metamask is an Open Source and Free Chrome Extension. This is a Web 3.0 Wallet that is used by millions of users worldwide to interact with various blockchains.
Why is Metamask needed for delphi.games?
Metamask is the completely trusted wallet that holds your Delphi Points for you. In effect, your Delphi points are stored on the blockchain, and only you control their deposit, withdrawal, transfer and redeeming for Gifts.
What are Delphi Points?
These are virtual points like your Credit Card points, that can be used across any games in Delphi Land. These can be used to redeem cool gifts, or buy other assets on the blockchain.
Can I buy Delphi points?
Every game is designed to be played in Public or Peer-to-Peer version. That is you can host a game, invite friends over Whatsapp and other media, and play along with them. The private games are also played with Delphi points and rewarded with Delphi points. Coolest Party in Town !
