WHATWE DO
 We bring Game Creators and Game Players on one platform, the technology of which is built on Blockchain/Web 3.0. The transparency and trustability of Blockchain ensure unconditional trust, without human intervention, in the flow of monetary value in the ecosystem.
WHAT
YOU GET
 Delphi Points, the currency of Delphi Land, your entry passes to all the games. Your first Game is on us!

The Delphi points are virtual points (like Credit Card points) and cannot be bought with a regular currency like Rupee or Dollar. Instead, it is earned by winning Games of Skill, referring friends, and executing skill-based Tasks.
